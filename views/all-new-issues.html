Welcome to Longbox

Keeping track of comics is hard. With hundreds of new issues coming out every week and release schedules that can change at any moment, knowing what you'll be picking up at your local comic shop on any given Wednesday has always been a mess. Longbox is here to make things easier. Utilizing data from Marvel and Comic Vine, you can maintain a subscription list of series you care about. This circumvents the need to sift through long lists from Previews or Diamond, cluttered by series you don't really care about. By using Longbox, you can quickly see which of the series you read are being released and which have been delayed, all from within your web browser, tablet or phone. Sign up today and never miss another issue.
